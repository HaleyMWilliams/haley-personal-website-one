About Haley

Haley Williams is a Front End Developer with experience using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React and more looking to continue building her career in tech. She is hardworking, passionate and enthusiastic, and is guaranteed to deliver results beyond expectations.

Following the acquisition of her Bachelor of Science in Human Communication from the University of Central Florida in 2017, Haley decided to follow her lifelong dream of moving to New York City to pursue a career in fashion. Fast forward through three years of working in both the fashion and retail industries, Haley found her true passion in coding and development.

Since beginning her coding journey in June of 2020, Haley has had the opportunity to design and build numerous front end and fullstack web applications, both independently and under freelance/consultant contracts, allowing her to bring clients' and personal visions to life, work closely with fellow developers, gain experience contributing to software for a seed stage startup, as well as flex her creative muscles, along with her analytical and problem solving skills. This experience has led her to pursue a career in Front End Development, an opportunity that allows her to utilize her wide range of strengths and interests.

Haley is passionate about this new chapter of her professional life and is eager to contribute her skills and grow with a company full time within the tech space. With an industry that is in a constant state of change, she is enthusiastic and eager to learn new coding languages, continue building upon her existing coding knowledge, hone her skills and work hard to become the best developer she can be.

Let's get coding!
