About Haley

Haley Williams is a recent SheCodes Plus React graduate whom is excited to utilize her new skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and more by beginning a career in tech. She is hardworking, passionate and enthusiastic, and is guaranteed to deliver results beyond expectations.

After earning her Bachelor of Science in Human Communication from the University of Central Florida in 2017, Haley decided to follow her lifelong dream of moving to New York City to pursue a career in fashion. Fast forward through three years of working in both the fashion and retail industries, Haley found her true passion in coding.

Through SheCodes Plus React coding bootcamp, Haley had the opportunity to design and build a weather app - in both JavaScript and React - allowing her to flex her creative muscles, and further explore her analytical and problem solving skills. This experience has led Haley to pursue a career in Front End Web Development, an opportunity that allows her to utilize her wide range of strengths and interests.

Haley looks forward to this new chapter of her professional life and is eager to begin a career in the tech space. With an industry that is in a constant state of change, Haley is enthusiastic and eager to learn new coding languages, continue building upon her existing coding knowledge, hone her skills and work hard to become the best developer she can be.
